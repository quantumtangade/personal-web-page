Shrikant Tangade received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering (Ramaiah Institute of Technology-MSRIT, Bengaluru), Master of Technology degree in Computer Network Engineering (R. V. College of Engineering, Bengaluru), and Ph. D. degree in Faculty of Electrical & Electronics Engineering Sciences, VTU, India in 2007, 2010, and 2020 respectively.

He is currently Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Mathematics, University of Padova, Italy (2022-2024), working in the SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group and Adjunct Professor, University of Padova, Italy.

He is working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bengaluru, India. He is Secretary of IEEE Standard P1920.2 Working Group (V2V Communications for Unmanned Aircraft Systems).
